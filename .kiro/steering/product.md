# Product Overview

NexaSuit is a comprehensive CRM and business management mobile application built with React Native and Expo. The app provides lead management, client relationship management, task tracking, meeting scheduling, and email communication features.

## Core Features

- **Lead Management**: Track prospects, convert to clients, manage lead interactions
- **Client Management**: Comprehensive client profiles with contact info, location data, and status tracking
- **Task Management**: Client-specific task tracking and organization
- **Meeting Scheduling**: Calendar integration with Google Calendar support
- **Email Communication**: Built-in email composer with templates and AI suggestions
- **Analytics & Reporting**: Business insights and performance tracking
- **Subscription Management**: Freemium model with usage limits and premium features

## Target Users

Business professionals, sales teams, and small to medium businesses who need mobile-first CRM capabilities with offline support and real-time synchronization.

## Key Differentiators

- Mobile-first design optimized for on-the-go business management
- AI-powered suggestions for email composition and lead interactions
- Integrated mapping and location services for field work
- Voice recording capabilities for meeting notes and client interactions
- Subscription-based feature gating with usage tracking
